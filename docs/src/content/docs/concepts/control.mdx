Control is the operational boundary layer for ThinkWork. It answers two blunt questions:

- what are agents allowed to do
- what actually happened when they did it

That means Control is not just safety policy. It is also spend management, visibility, and auditability.

## What Control covers

In the current open source docs, Control includes:

- guardrails at model invocation time
- budgets and usage tracking
- rate limits and spend boundaries
- audit logging in your AWS account

## Why this matters

As soon as agents move from demos into real work, you need a way to enforce boundaries and inspect behavior after the fact. ThinkWork treats that as a first-class concept instead of an afterthought.
